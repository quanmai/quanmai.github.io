Understanding Things By Writing

I realize that reading papers and materials is not enough to understand a subject deeply, writing things out, on the other hand, helps me to understand things better. Putting into words what I have in mind helps clarify my thoughts and identify gaps in my understanding. This page is a collection of my thoughts on various topics, some might be taken from papers that I read, some might be my own ideas, and some might be inspired by popcasts that I listened to. Read at your own risk (of being bored lol).
