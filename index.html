Quan Mai

I am pursuing my PhD degree in Computer Engineering at University of Arkansas at Fayettville.

I mainly work on machine learning, deep learning and high performance computing. I am currently working on Graph Neural Networks and their applications in Medical Imaging and Routing Problems.

I do not consider myself as a naturally good coder, I thereby practise coding as a hobby in my sparetime. Python is the language that I choose to have fun in, although I have done lots of projects in C++.

Previously, I was a Super-compute Architect intern at Intel Corporation (Spring 2022) and a IP Design Engineer at Synopsys VietNam (2016-2019).
